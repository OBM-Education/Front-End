Third Success Story

“Alone, we can do so little; together we can do so much”
- Helen Keller
This is Akshat’s quote.

“My goal to study at a global university was rooted in the many extra-curricular activities that exposed me to the unexplored realms of internationalism, community, liberal education, and more.” He worked a lot on himself to reach his goal, got involved in so many activities in order to learn, Akshat didn’t work only on the technical part of himself.

He continued saying: “Winning innovation and science fairs helped me think out of the box while Model UNs and Debates polished my communication and critical thinking abilities.”

“BP Global STEM Academy Brazil led me to build amazing connections with individuals worldwide and Round Square International Conference empowered me to succeed at leadership in large, international environments. Juggling academics while trying out new experiences and activities definitely is hard and requires strong will but if you're enjoying what you're doing, then it's all worth it. I'm grateful to now be attending a university whose mission and values resonate with me and empowers me to expand my knowledge and perspectives every day. “

The source of giving back to the high school community now is through YouTube @akshutup

He aims to provide all the help students can ask for through the channel he started worked on recently.

Full funded scholarship in New York University, Participant in BP Global STEM Academies Brazil 2019, Member in Model United Nations.
