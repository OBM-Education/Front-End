OBM's mission is to promote the new youth generation by needed soft and hard skills to be qualified for the labor market even as an employee or entrepreneur, support students to decide their major of study, and lead the youth through their academic journey and career path by holding events, programs and sessions while being their required guide in their age group.
OBM Egypt aim helping and qualifying the high school students for the college life and guiding them through their admission process in addition to helping them how to go for the job-hunting route and how to choose the right & suitable career through the activities, programs and summits that is held by OBM Egypt like "CPP" and The Largest Summit for High School Students & Scholarships in Egypt "BYF".
