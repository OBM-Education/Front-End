The EducationUSA Competitive College Club
CCC.

The EducationUSA Competitive College Club (CCC) is an intensive group advising program that assists top high school students who want to be competitive applicants to U.S. colleges and universities. Students meet year-round and participate in lectures, book clubs, community service activities, cross-cultural exercises and test preparation, all conducted in English.

The activities help ensure that students are properly advised as they go through the process of preparing themselves both academically and socially for a U.S. college or university, and work to determine which schools might be a good fit.

OBM CCC TimeLine .

01
The first semester
It will be only on the highlights of the application, what they need on their long-term plan, and what to start immediately, There will be two lectures every week and one of them alone for every candidate:-
  • The first one will discuss the application process and different things they must know about as Competitions, fields, majors, and help them to decide their field of study.
  • The private meeting will discuss the progress of the candidate.
  • There will be a vacation of two weeks after every semester.
02
The second semester
It will be held as the same Timeline (Two Lectures every week), but more specified as they have the standard basics, and will apply all they know about Competitions and clubs by participating.
03
The third semester
It will be held as the same Timeline (Two Lectures every week), and more on this semester in this specific semester as they will decide their standardized tests and start to prepare for them.
04
The fourth-Fifth semester
It will discuss the application process and Essays more specifically and practice on the application writings. Finally start their application in the fifth semester.
